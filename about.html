About Ascend

Our History

Ascend Baruch was founded in 2005 as the first student chapter of Ascend Nationals, and it continues to be one of the largest Pan-Asian business organization on campus today. With a growing number of 300 and more members, Ascend Baruch promotes self-development to the student community by providing leadership opportunities, mentorship programs, and skills-enhancing workshops. We engage Baruch students with a variety of professional, social, and volunteer events throughout the academic year to lay the foundation for a meaningful and successful college experience.
