Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do I need to be Asian to join?

Nope, absolutely not! We are a very diverse organization that takes anyone who shows interest!

2. What is the GPA or major requirement?

There are no GPA or major requirements to become an Ascend member. We welcome all people who strive to better themselves as a student and a professional.

3. When is the last day to join Ascend?

There is no semester based application process. You can join right this instant!

4. Why should I join?

Look at all the benefits of being an Ascend member here!

5. I want to join but I don’t have a credit card, what should I do?

Simply contact one of the Ascend Baruch Executive Board leaders and we will gladly pay on your behalf while you pay us back in cash.

6. How do I apply to be one of your committees?

Come to one of our Ascend Members Meetings (AMMs) or contact one of the Ascend Baruch Executive Board leaders who leads the committee to express your interest. Check here for even more info!

7. When do you hold your meetings?

General Interest Meetings and Member Meetings are held every month. For exact dates, subscribe to our weekly newsletter and check out our calendar. ​

8. Where can I meet more Ascend members or where do members usually hang out?

Many of us are all over Baruch, however you can stop by our club room to meet people! Come to our club room in the Vertical Campus building. It is within the club area, so you may meet even more awesome people!​ ​

Ready to Ascend?

We welcome any questions you may have. Please leave your inquiry below and we will contact you as soon as possible.

You can also contact us through email at ascendbaruch@gmail.com or visit our club room 3-229.
